Welcome to BASHGO

BASHGO Maintenance Services Pvt Ltd was formed in 2004 as a small company specialising in Operation & Maintenance works for process industries in the area of Mechanical, Electrical and Instrumentation. Since that time, the company has grown into a medium sized contractor providing specialist services in all areas of utility infrastructure installation, operation and maintenance, Overhauling, Erection & Commissioning, etc.,. The company has a strong portfolio of clients including many process industries for which it has successfully rendering and completed contracts.
